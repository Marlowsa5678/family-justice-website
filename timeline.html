From first breakthrough to government vindication: How independent journalism changed systems

2009
🏥

Dr. Anna Chacko VA Termination: The Breakthrough

Volpe's first major success exposed Dr. Anna Chacko's systematic abuse of staff at Pittsburgh VA Hospital. This rare victory against VA bureaucracy launched his reputation as a journalist who gets results and established his methodology for taking on entrenched institutions.

First Major Victory: Chacko terminated January 2010, establishing precedent for removing disruptive VA physicians. Featured in Pittsburgh Tribune, launching Volpe's national recognition.
2012
📚
Book Publication

"Prosecutors Gone Wild"

October 4, 2012: Volpe's first book examining the corrupt trial of Chicago Heights officials Chuck Panici, John Gliottoni, and Louise Marshall. Established him as an author documenting systemic corruption for posterity.

Publishing Milestone: Transformed ephemeral journalism into lasting resource for corruption studies and reform advocates. Set template for future investigative books.
2013
⛪

Cardinal Wuerl Investigation: Five Years Ahead

In a prescient Crime Magazine exposé, Volpe revealed Cardinal Donald Wuerl's role in covering up pedophile priests—five years before the Pennsylvania grand jury reached similar conclusions. Demonstrated his ability to see patterns others missed.

Vindicated by History: Wuerl resigned as Archbishop of Washington D.C. in October 2018 when grand jury report mentioned him 200+ times, proving Volpe's early reporting.
2013
📰

Rosilyn Wells Obamacare Navigator Scandal

October 2013: During healthcare.gov's disastrous rollout, Volpe's Daily Caller investigation exposed an ACA navigator with an outstanding arrest warrant, becoming a national symbol of the program's security failures.

National Headlines: Featured on Drudge Report, amplified by congressional Republicans, highlighted critical security flaws in navigator program accessing sensitive personal data.
2014
📚
Book Publication

"The Definitive Dossier of PTSD in Whistleblowers"

January 24, 2014: Pioneering research documenting the psychological trauma experienced by whistleblowers. First comprehensive study linking PTSD to the act of exposing corruption and subsequent retaliation.

Academic Contribution: Advanced understanding of whistleblower psychology, influenced support programs, and provided scientific backing for whistleblower protection advocacy.
2014
🔍

Keith Dobbs Investigation Begins

Daily Caller exposé revealed Memphis attorney Keith Dobbs was systematically stealing from vulnerable veterans through the VA Fiduciary program. Launched multi-year investigation that would ultimately trigger federal action.

Long-term Impact: Investigation would culminate in 67-count federal indictment five years later, demonstrating Volpe's persistence in seeing complex cases through to justice.
2015
📚
Book Publication

"Bullied to Death: Chris Mackney's Kafkaesque Divorce"

August 20, 2015: Documented the tragic case of Chris Mackney, driven to suicide by family court abuse. Brought national attention to systematic problems in divorce and custody proceedings.

Family Court Reform: Became essential resource for family court reform advocates, helping families understand systematic abuse patterns and inspiring protective legislation.
2015
📺

Memphis VA Director Dismissed After O'Reilly Factor

Volpe's 2014-2015 investigative series on Memphis VA Medical Center mismanagement caught national attention when featured on The O'Reilly Factor, demonstrating how persistent reporting can force institutional change.

Institutional Reform: Hospital director dismissed, multiple department chiefs removed, facility received $16 million in new equipment and showed marked patient care improvements.
2016
📚
Book Publication

"Sandra Grazzini-Rucki and the World's Last Custody Trial"

October 6, 2016: Co-authored with attorney Michelle MacDonald, providing the truth when mainstream media including ABC's 20/20 painted a false narrative about a protective mother fighting family court corruption.

Media Counter-Narrative: Minnesota Court of Appeals later overturned Sandra's "cruel and unusual" sentence, vindicating Volpe's reporting against establishment media bias.
2019
⚖️

Keith Dobbs: 67 Federal Charges

Volpe's 2014 investigation culminated in a 67-count federal indictment against Keith Dobbs for stealing from veterans. Included 26 counts of mail fraud and 22 counts of embezzling VA benefits, with 35 victims identified.

Justice Delivered: $406,533 in criminal proceeds sought, Dobbs disbarred and faced potential life imprisonment. Established Volpe's reputation for investigations that produce concrete legal consequences.
2019
🏛️

Orange County Family Court Corruption Investigation

Began exposing Orange County family court corruption involving child abuse cover-ups, false "parental alienation" labels against protective parents, and private judges favoring connected lawyers and psychologists. Documented systematic corruption affecting vulnerable families.

Ongoing Exposure: Continuing investigation revealing network of court actors profiting from prolonged custody battles while children suffer abuse.
2021
💰

Cherokee County DSS: $48.5 Million Settlement

Exposed systemic corruption in North Carolina's Cherokee County Department of Social Services involving illegal child custody practices. Investigation led to multiple felony indictments against officials including former DSS director Cindy Palmer.

Record Settlement: $48.5 million settlement approved by county commissioners—one of the largest corruption settlements in recent memory, with statewide policy changes implemented.
2021
👨‍⚖️

St. Louis County: Judge Forced to Retire

Secret Zoom video of nearly 40 corrupt Guardian ad Litems led to investigation exposing Presiding Judge Michael Burton's inappropriate ex-parte emails. Burton called lawsuit allegations "scurrilous" while describing parents as "toxic" and "unhinged."

Unprecedented Judicial Impact: Burton retired August 2021, all 31 judges in St. Louis County Circuit Court forced to recuse themselves, international headlines via PJ Media coverage.
2021
🎖️

Army Ranger Freed from Corrupt Guardianship

Through Communities Digital News series, exposed how a 37-year-old former Army Ranger was trapped in a corrupt guardianship system designed to exploit vulnerable adults for profit.

Legal Precedent: Veteran freed, established legal precedent for challenging corrupt guardianships, brought national attention to guardianship abuse affecting thousands.
Ongoing

The Elaine Pudlowski Act: Nationwide Family Court Reform

Volpe authored groundbreaking proposed legislation to eliminate rogue custody evaluators and Guardian ad Litems from family courts. His extensive investigations into Connecticut and other state family court corruption influenced reform efforts across multiple states.

Legislative Influence: Arizona's HB 2256 banning court-appointed therapeutic interventionists passed both legislative chambers; family court reform movement spreading nationwide.
2025
🏛️

Government Vindication: Official State Hearings

June 2025: Arizona state government hearings on family court corruption. July 2025: Idaho state government hearings. Sixteen years of Volpe's investigations and advocacy culminate in official government acknowledgment and action on systematic family court abuse.

Ultimate Vindication: State governments now officially investigating the very corruption Volpe has exposed for over a decade. His independent journalism directly influenced government reform efforts, proving the power of persistent truth-telling.
