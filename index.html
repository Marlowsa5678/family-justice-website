About Michael Volpe

Michael Volpe is a freelance investigative journalist and author based in Chicago, specializing in exposing court corruption, particularly within the family court system. After working in finance for over a decade, he transitioned to investigative journalism in 2009.

Journalism Focus: Volpe's work exposes judicial misconduct and corruption, with particular emphasis on parental alienation and the devastating impacts of family court systems on individuals and families. He has been recognized by many whistleblowers for helping bring their stories to light.

Published Work: His investigative journalism has appeared in major national publications including The Daily Caller, The American Conservative, Washington Examiner, and Crime Magazine, as well as prominent Chicago media outlets.

Author & Advocate: Volpe has authored multiple books on court corruption and actively advocates for family court reform, even proposing legislation like the Elaine Pudlowski Act to address systemic issues.

Primary Platform: Substack - where he publishes ongoing investigations and commentary.
