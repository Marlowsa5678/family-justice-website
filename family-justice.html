🎯 Take Action: Get Your State Committee Started

Copy this message and send it to your state representatives:

Subject: Request for Family Court Corruption Committee Dear [Representative Name], I am writing to request that our state establish a Family Court Corruption Committee to conduct public hearings, similar to the successful committees already established in Arizona and Idaho. Arizona has held multiple days of public testimony from families affected by family court corruption, and Idaho recently conducted their first hearing on July 7, 2025. These committees are providing crucial oversight and reform recommendations. Families in our state deserve the same opportunity to be heard and to see meaningful reforms implemented. I respectfully request that you: 1. Introduce legislation to establish a Family Court Corruption Committee 2. Schedule public hearings for affected families to testify 3. Work toward implementing the reforms that come from these hearings Thank you for your consideration of this important matter. Sincerely, [Your Name] [Your Address] [Your Phone] [Your Email]
